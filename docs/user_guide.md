# 📖 FRIDAY: User Experience Guide

Welcome to **FRIDAY**.

Most digital assistants wait for you to ask them a question. FRIDAY is different. It is an ambient, empathetic digital partner that lives across your phone and laptop. It understands your context, protects your focus, and rebuilds your workspace so you can stay in your "flow state" without cognitive overload.

Here is how to get the most out of your FRIDAY ecosystem.

---

## 1. Getting Started: The Daily Routine

Because FRIDAY protects your privacy by processing everything locally, you need to ensure your devices are talking to each other.

1. **Start your Laptop Hub:** Open your terminal and run the FRIDAY backend. (This wakes up your local AI brain).
2. **Open the Android App:** Launch FRIDAY on your phone. It will automatically discover your laptop over the local Wi-Fi and connect silently in the background.
3. **Focus on your work:** That’s it. Put your phone down. FRIDAY is now actively shielding your attention.

---

## 2. Core Features & How They Work

### 🧘‍♂️ Empathetic Silence (Focus Protection)

**What it does:** Have you ever been deep into a difficult coding problem or essay, only to be interrupted by five buzzing group chat messages?
**How you use it:** You don't have to do anything. FRIDAY passively monitors your typing pace and active windows. If it detects you are in a state of high focus or high cognitive load, it enforces **Empathetic Silence**. It intercepts non-urgent notifications on your phone, silences them, and holds them for you.

### 📝 The Contextual Digest

**What it does:** When you finally take a break and pick up your phone, you won't see a stressful wall of 50 missed notifications.
**How you use it:** Unlock your phone after a long work session. FRIDAY will instantly generate a 2-3 sentence AI summary of everything you missed (e.g., *"Soumya sent 4 messages about the hackathon database, and you have a calendar reminder for 3:00 PM."*).

### 🔄 Semantic Workspace Restoration

**What it does:** Moving from your phone back to your laptop usually means spending 5 minutes re-opening the tabs, PDFs, and notes you need.
**How you use it:** 1. When you sit back down at your laptop, click the **FRIDAY Chrome Extension**.
2. FRIDAY remembers what task you were working on before you left (e.g., "Neural Ethics Research").
3. Click the **Restore Flow** button. FRIDAY will instantly side-load your exact cluster of related tabs and documents, dropping you right back into the zone.

### 👻 Ghost Mode (Privacy & Burnout Shield)

**What it does:** FRIDAY actively measures your behavioral biometrics—specifically, your typing rhythm and backspace frequency.
**How you use it:** * **If you are burning out:** If your typing becomes erratic and error-prone, FRIDAY assumes you are highly stressed. The extension will toggle into **Ghost Mode** (shifting your screen to a dark 'Block Navy' theme), hide distracting tabs, and suggest you step away.

* **If someone else uses your laptop:** Because their typing rhythm doesn't match yours, FRIDAY instantly triggers Ghost Mode, locking down your sensitive workspace memory until you return.

### 🎙️ Sub-Second Voice Commands

**What it does:** Hands-free control that actually responds instantly.
**How you use it:** Simply say the wake-word **"FRIDAY"** near your phone, followed by your request (e.g., *"FRIDAY, remind me to email Thomas sir about the purchase order"*). Because it uses a local C++ transcription engine, it processes your voice in milliseconds without sending your audio to the cloud.

---

## 3. Understanding the Visual Interface

FRIDAY uses color psychology on your laptop extension to communicate its current state without requiring you to read text:

* 🪻 **Block Lilac (Aware Mode):** FRIDAY is active, learning your context, and routing data normally. Your environment is relaxed.
* 🌑 **Block Navy (Ghost Mode):** FRIDAY has detected stress, erratic typing, or an unrecognized user. The interface goes dark, and non-essential tabs are hidden to protect you.
* 🟢 **Block Lime (Interaction Mode):** A proactive UI card requires your attention, such as asking if you want to restore a workspace cluster.

---

## 4. Troubleshooting & Offline Mode

**"What happens if my Wi-Fi drops or I leave my house?"**
You never have to worry about FRIDAY crashing or losing your data:

* **Network Switching:** If you switch from Wi-Fi to 5G, FRIDAY automatically bridges the connection to your laptop via a secure tunnel.
* **Total Offline Fallback:** If you are on an airplane or your laptop dies, the Android app seamlessly routes your data to its own **on-device local AI (Phi-3 Mini)**. FRIDAY will continue to generate summaries and log your context, syncing back to your laptop the moment you reconnect.
